About This Demo

This page demonstrates the embeddable feedback widget that can be added to any website. The widget allows visitors to:

  • Submit their name, email, and phone number
  • Rate their experience with 1-5 stars
  • Leave detailed comments
  • Automatically get added to your newsletter

How to Embed on Your Website

Simply add this one line of code before the closing </body> tag on any webpage:

<script src="https://your-domain.com/widget/feedback.js"></script>

Features

  • Automatic Email Notifications: Both you and the user receive emails
  • Newsletter Integration: Users are automatically added to your mailing list
  • Admin Dashboard: View all submissions at /admin/feedback
  • Mobile Responsive: Works perfectly on all devices
  • Easy to Customize: Matches your brand colors
